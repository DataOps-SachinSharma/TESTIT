12. Compensatory Off (Comp Off) Policy

12.1 Purpose

Defines eligibility, approval process, utilization, and compliance requirements for Compensatory Off granted to employees who work on company-declared holidays or weekends due to business requirements.

12.2 Eligibility

Employees are eligible for Comp Off when:

  • Work is performed on a company-declared holiday or weekend.
  • Prior approval has been obtained from the Reporting Manager before performing the work.
  • The work is required for business continuity, project deadlines, customer commitments, production support, or other organizational needs.

Not eligible:

  • Voluntary work performed without prior managerial approval.
  • Work performed outside normal working hours on regular working days.
  • Incomplete or unsupported Comp Off requests.

12.3 Approval Process

  1. Pre-approval Get approval from your Reporting Manager before working on a holiday or weekend if you intend to claim Comp Off.
  2. Submit the request After completing the approved work, email your Reporting Manager with: date worked, holiday/weekend worked, project/task details, duration of work, and business justification.
  3. CC the required recipients Jaimin Shah, Utsav Surana, Suraj Patel, and Pallvi Vij (HR).
  4. Manager review The Reporting Manager reviews and approves/rejects within 3 working days of submission.
  5. Apply on TMS Portal For the Comp Off application, log in to the TMS Portal and submit your request.TMS Portal
Email checklist — To: Reporting Manager · CC: Jaimin Shah, Utsav Surana, Suraj Patel, Pallvi Vij (HR).

12.4 Usage of Comp Off

  • Must be availed within 30 calendar days from the date the additional work was performed.
  • Unused Comp Off automatically lapses after 30 days.
  • Apply through the TMS Portal and get it approved by your Reporting Manager before utilization.
  • Should not adversely impact project deliverables or business operations.

Combination with other leave types: Comp Off cannot be combined with Earned Leave (EL), Casual Leave (CL), Sick Leave (SL), or any other leave category unless explicitly approved by the Reporting Manager.

Encashment: Comp Off cannot be converted into cash or monetary compensation under any circumstances.

12.5 Maximum Limit

  • Maximum 3 Comp Off days per calendar month.
  • Maximum 2 consecutive Comp Off days at a time.
  • Additional requests require special approval from Management.

12.6 Exceptions & Special Cases

Management reserves the right to modify approved Comp Off schedules, revoke or defer approvals due to project-critical activities, or request rescheduling. Any exception requires approval from Senior Management.

12.7 Policy Compliance

Disciplinary action may result from: submission of false or misleading information, misrepresentation of working hours, unauthorized Comp Off claims, or manipulation of attendance records. The Company may conduct periodic audits of Comp Off requests and supporting records.

12.8 Effective Date

This policy is effective immediately and remains in force until amended, replaced, or withdrawn by Management. GTRTeK LLC reserves the right to review, revise, interpret, or modify this policy at any time based on business, legal, or organizational needs.
