Employee Health Insurance & Dependent Coverage

GTRTeK LLC provides comprehensive health insurance coverage for employees and their families. This page covers eligibility, dependent enrollment, coverage guidelines and your responsibilities.

Policy Coverage

The company-sponsored Health Insurance Plan covers eligible employees and their immediate dependents:

  • Employee
  • Legally married spouse
  • Children

Coverage for eligible dependents is subject to submission and verification of required supporting documents.

Parent Coverage (Optional)

  • Parent coverage is optional and can be added on request.
  • Additional premium costs are borne by the employee and deducted from monthly salary.
  • Premium amounts vary based on age, insurer guidelines and selected coverage.

For more information, contact Shashi Gowda or Pallvi Vij.

Annual Enrollment & Updates

During each enrollment period, employees must review their dependent information and submit additions or modifications within the communicated timeline. If no updates are received by the deadline, GTRTeK LLC proceeds with the information currently on file.

Employee Responsibilities

  • Provide accurate dependent information.
  • Submit required supporting documents.
  • Inform HR of any family status changes.
  • Review insurance details during renewal periods.
  • Keep dependent information current.

Inaccurate or incomplete information may result in delays, claim rejection, or coverage limitations imposed by the insurance provider.

Confidentiality & Data Protection

All employee and dependent information collected for insurance purposes is treated as confidential and shared only with authorized insurance providers, administrators and company representatives involved in benefit administration.
