Recreational Benefits

Employees can use recreational benefits after completing 6 months of probation — up to ₹30,000 for entertainment and health appliances.

Overview

Recreational benefits cover electronic appliances for entertainment and health appliances. Bills must be GST-authenticated and computerised, sent to your manager with HR in CC.

Eligible Items

CategoryExamples
Electronic appliances (entertainment)Mobile, laptop, tablet, speaker, gaming consoles (e.g. PS4), smartwatch, etc.
Health appliancesSmart fitness headbands, smart digital thermometer, blood pressure monitor, meditation gadgets, pulse oximeter, digital glucometer, etc.
Not covered: AC, TV, and fridge are currently excluded from the recreational benefits category.

Claim Rules

  • An authenticated GST bill is required for every claim.
  • Payment must be via bank transaction from your own account — UPI, NEFT, RTGS, IMPS and similar digital transactions are accepted.
  • Cash-based transaction bills are not accepted.
  • Send the claim to your Reporting Manager, with HR in CC.
  • Total allowance: ₹30,000 per employee.

Claim Form — Fields to Prepare

The Reimbursement Form for Recreational Benefits (full-time employees only) asks for:

#Field
1Name
2Designation
3Employee ID
4Reporting Manager / Department Head / Immediate Supervisor
5Number of days' vacation taken
6Total amount to be reimbursed
7Dates of absenteeism when the vacation was taken
8Journey details: receipt no. & GSTIN, mode of travel, departure/arrival date-time, from/to, fare paid, distance (km) for road mileage
9Accommodation: period of stay, hotel name, GSTIN, daily rate, total amount paid
10Other recreational activities (food, lodging, gym, movies): receipt no. & GSTIN, date, purpose, receipt name, relationship to receipt holder, amount
