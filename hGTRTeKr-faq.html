Company Laptop Issuance (Post-Probation)

Eligibility: 6 months of probation completed, plus your manager's approval.
  1. Prepare a confirmation document Include your name, designation, employee ID, confirmation that probation is complete, and a request for a laptop as per company policy.
  2. Get manager approval Share the document with your reporting manager — an email or Teams chat confirmation is fine.
  3. Email HR / Admin Send the approved document, mention the laptop requirement, and reference the manager's approval.
  4. Laptop allocation & agreement The Admin Team processes the request and issues a Laptop Agreement Form.
  5. Acknowledge and submit Read the agreement, fill in your details, sign it, and submit the signed copy to HR/Admin.

Note: the laptop is company property, used per IT policy. On transfer, resignation, or internal movement, it must be returned or reissued officially.

Career Development

What growth opportunities are available within the company?

We encourage employees to pursue growth through internal job postings, mentorship programs, and cross-functional projects. Promotions are based on merit and alignment with business needs.

Are there training or certification programs to enhance skills?

Yes — access to online learning platforms, in-house training sessions, and reimbursement for certifications aligned with your role.

How can I align my career goals with organisational objectives?

Discuss with your manager to create an Individual Development Plan (IDP), aligning your aspirations with the company's goals.

Performance Feedback & Review

How can I improve my performance and contribution to the team?

Regularly request feedback from your manager and peers, set measurable goals, and take ownership of your tasks. Performance review cycles provide structured opportunities for improvement.

Are there mentorship programs or peer learning opportunities?

Yes — a mentorship program pairs experienced professionals with junior team members, plus regular team-based knowledge-sharing sessions.

What are best practices for advancing within the organisation?

Consistency, innovation, collaboration, and taking initiative are key. Demonstrating leadership and achieving your goals helps you advance.

Preparing for your performance review

As part of the annual review process, prepare a brief one- or two-page self-assessment summarising your achievements and their impact. Keep it simple and crisp. Consider:

  • Achievements & productivity — projects completed in the past 12 months and your most significant accomplishments.
  • Challenges — which aspects of your job have been most challenging.
  • Goals for the coming year — your primary objectives and responsibilities ahead.
  • Performance improvement — actions that could help, by you or with support from your manager.
  • Career aspirations — where you'd like to be in one, two, or five years.
  • Skill development — skills or experiences you'd like to build next year.

Company Policies

What's the policy on flexible working hours or remote work?

Our flexible work policy allows remote or hybrid arrangements, subject to managerial approval and role requirements.

How does the company handle workplace diversity and inclusion?

Through recruitment practices, workshops on bias awareness, and an open-door policy for reporting concerns.

What are the guidelines for professional conduct and conflict resolution?

A code of ethics emphasising respect, teamwork and professionalism. Employees are encouraged to report conflicts to HR for mediation.

Work Environment & Culture

What initiatives promote a healthy work-life balance?

Wellness programs, flexible working hours, mental health resources, and frequent employee engagement activities.

Are there upcoming company-wide events or team-building activities?

Yes — quarterly team-building sessions and annual company retreats. Watch the company calendar for dates.

How can I contribute to improving workplace culture?

By actively participating in company initiatives, giving constructive feedback, and embracing teamwork and inclusivity.

General Support

What resources are available for employees dealing with personal challenges?

Employee Assistance Programs (EAPs), counselling services, and wellness workshops.

Who should I contact for harassment or discrimination issues?

Report incidents to HR immediately. All reports are treated confidentially and investigated thoroughly.

How can HR help with interpersonal conflicts within teams?

HR can act as a neutral mediator, facilitating discussions to reach solutions that work for all parties.
