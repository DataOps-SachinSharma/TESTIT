Standard Leave & Holiday Policy

Effective year: 2026 · Applies to all full-time, permanent GTRTeK LLC employees across all locations, unless a location-specific policy states otherwise.

This document is also referenced by GTRTeK HR AI Agent, the company's virtual HR assistant, when answering employee leave questions. Leave entitlements may vary by statutory location — use the location filter in AIVA or the HR portal for location-specific detail.

1–2. Purpose & Scope

This policy outlines the standard leave entitlements and national holidays applicable to all eligible employees of GTRTeK LLC for calendar year 2026, so employees can understand their leave benefits and plan time off accordingly.

5. General Guidelines

  • All leave requests must be submitted and approved in advance through the TMS Portal, except in emergencies (e.g. sick leave, bereavement leave).
  • Unused Earned Leave (EL) may be carried forward as per the carry-forward limit defined by HR; leave beyond the limit will lapse unless otherwise approved.
  • Maternity and Paternity Leave are governed by applicable statutory regulations and may require supporting documentation.
  • Loss of Pay (LOP) is applied only after all other applicable leave balances have been exhausted, and is subject to manager approval.
  • Birthday Leave must be availed within the same calendar month as the employee's birthday.
  • Marital Leave applies to the employee's own wedding and must be requested at least 2 weeks in advance where possible.

6. How to Apply for Leave

Employees can apply for leave through the TMS Portal or by asking GTRTeK HR AI Agent directly — for example, "How do I apply for casual leave?" AIVA will guide you through the applicable process based on the leave type selected.
