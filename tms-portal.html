TMS Portal — Timesheet Management System

The TMS Portal is the single place to apply for leave, submit timecards, and track approvals. For any leave application or timecard submission, use the portal and also inform your manager on Teams.

Portal home: portal.gtrtek.com  ·  TMS home: tms.gtrtek.com

Documents

Payslips and other HR documents are available in the portal's Documents section. To generate or download a payslip, use the Documents tab, or email pallvi.vij@gtrtek.com.

Routing rule: for leave applications, always apply via the TMS Portal. For mail approvals, send to your Reporting Manager — or Project Manager if you're on a cross-project assignment.
